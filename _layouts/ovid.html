Available Everywhere, All the Time

Ideas strike when you least expect them. Record them all with the plaintext editor a tab away. Available on all devices.

Features

  • Autosave
  • Font selection (serif, sans-serif, monospace)
  • Light / Dark theme
  • Customizable Margins
  • Word Count, Date and Time toolbar
  • Progressive Web App
  • Free and Open Source

On the Desktop

You can install Ovid as an app on your dock. Simply select "Add to dock" or "Install App" from your favorite web browser's options menu.

Your Tools, Your Rules

Ovid leverages your operating system's word processing capabilities. Spelling and text editing preferences are set according to your device's language and keyboard configurations. With Ovid, your writing is right at home.

You can integrate Ovid with Vivaldi as a side panel app, that way you can take notes on any website.
